<p>Completed in March 2007, the Agape Concert Hall is an ideal venue for
concerts, plays, seminars, conferences and award ceremonies. The concert
hall boasts state-of-art acoustics and is fully-equipped for live recording
and multi-media presentations.</p>
<p>At the entrance is a spacious foyer. Backstage is well-equipped with dressing
rooms and stage management facilities. The seating capacity is capped at
771. Each guest will enjoy an equally visually and aurally appealing perspective
from any angle.</p>
