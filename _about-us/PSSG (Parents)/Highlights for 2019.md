### PL Amazing Race

<iframe allowfullscreen="true" height="569" width="960" frameborder="0" src="https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/e/2PACX-1vRWVqH9q6YFt8lCynAVejvEuXtAP2DQk9TOmdBpYMPCnrT-sXP4EnfRC6CYjQZCsuUcePlTG5uTZSM5/embed?start=true&amp;loop=true&amp;delayms=3000"></iframe>

Besides racing round the island to get to the various check-points, they soaked in the sights and sounds of Sentosa. It was a morning filled with fun, laughter and sweat, bonding with the PL family!

### Cyberwellness Workshop cum Health Talk

<iframe src="https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/e/2PACX-1vT8OuDtOqPygadSNkJ4BLBXFLxS1NRgLmqgKJgnewaXVP0uFlfHTl2HyFawdfL9sV5StQgiUhPwqBUR/embed?start=true&amp;loop=true&amp;delayms=3000" frameborder="0" width="960" height="569" allowfullscreen="true"></iframe>

Thereafter, they also learnt about healthy eating habits that they can adopt as a family after a talk conducted by Health Promotion Board.  

### Breakfast Bonding with School Leaders cum Learning Festival

<iframe allowfullscreen="true" height="569" width="960" frameborder="0" src="https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/e/2PACX-1vRtVdfLh5KmJdC76ljpBgAEg9kCiu2rU7WOV1bzp61yZ2MOSNHgWW4LedRW6S0SyriBDzZAfRuFL-VO/embed?start=true&amp;loop=true&amp;delayms=3000"></iframe>

Over a hundred parents turned up on 9 March to attend the annual dialogue with PL’s school leaders and also for workshops to understand the current O-level and N-level subjects’ requirement and to learn skills to support their daughters in their character and academic development. The parents had a deeper understanding of Emotional Quotient and Social Quotient in the workshop conducted by Mr Halbert Louis from Connect Consultancy Training. Following that, the parents attended various sessions on academic areas, like PL 21, Oral Skills, Mathematics and Humanities, helmed by the various departments.

### CNY Lunch for the Elderly

<iframe allowfullscreen="true" height="569" width="960" frameborder="0" src="https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/e/2PACX-1vSqy-mJG4DOCEvUEto05dAhyGWE1bbsVUdN5CT4Mn4s0WVHaBLCy8MPBR3w669F7_6DyQoRWuf1WHoO/embed?start=true&amp;loop=true&amp;delayms=3000"></iframe>

On 19 Feb 2019, the PSSG parents jointly hosted the elderly invited by the school to a Chinese New Year lunch as part of the Secondary 4 and Secondary 5 Values-in-Action programme. The parents interacted with the elderly and made them feel loved and appreciated. It has a joyous occasion as we celebrated CNY as one big PL family!

### CNY Love Letter Making

<iframe allowfullscreen="true" height="569" width="960" frameborder="0" src="https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/e/2PACX-1vSozZQSQXu2zNNEoM9HCxlcbpzej86Lu-P9Gwr0xUGUY_aSTHXHWso6QJOPrFSjG4FVjd7fymGhBh59/embed?start=true&amp;loop=true&amp;delayms=3000"></iframe>

To enrich our students’ experience of Chinese New Year, our enthusiastic parents were roped in to teach PL-Lites the traditional way of making love letters over charcoal stoves throughout the respective recesses and lunch periods. Though it was a tiring and hot activity on 13 February, the smiles on the students’ faces gave the parents a sense of satisfaction and happiness. The parents also bonded with their daughters and their friends over making traditional love letter together.  

### CNY Dinner for International Students

<iframe allowfullscreen="true" height="569" width="960" frameborder="0" src="https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/e/2PACX-1vQmW_YJdHIG41iBxY1xNPEWvaOAQK8CXbQcyMpMY5LLxUz3DyEuNBcGZR2kOT7bzn4Y6ScCKZRKvu0E/embed?start=true&amp;loop=true&amp;delayms=3000"></iframe>

On 15 Feb 2019 evening, parents from our PSSG organised a Chinese New Year dinner for our International students and their local buddies. The students had a scrumptious dinner at the NE corner in school followed by a fun time playing with sparklers. It was a good family time for our international students as they bonded with their local buddies and PSSG parents. The students showed their appreciation by presenting a rendition of CNY songs, much to the delight of the parents!
