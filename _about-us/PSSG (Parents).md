<h2>PL Parent-School Support Group (PSSG)</h2>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/pssg_new_logo.png">
</div>
<h4>Our Vision:</h4>
<p>An exciting and effective partnership between home and school</p>
<h4>Our Mission:</h4>
<p>To <strong>LINK</strong> up all parents to achieve the school’s mission.</p>
<p>[<strong>LINK</strong> – <strong>L</strong>inking <strong>I</strong>ntentionally
through <strong>N</strong>etworking and <strong>K</strong>oinonia]</p>
<h4>Our Purpose:</h4>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>To provide support in various ways to help the school achieve its mission.
<br>
</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To raise awareness of the school’s activities and events to other parents.
<br>
</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To encourage parents to contribute their expertise and resources to the
school.
<br>
</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To serve as a feedback channel between the parents and the school
<br>
</p>
</li>
</ol>
<h4>Our focus areas:</h4>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>To support school</p>
</li>
</ol>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>As partners in school events, eg year-end strategic planning, talk at
staff meet, career talks for the graduating students
<br>
</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>As chaperone for learning journeys
<br>
</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>As judges for school events
<br>
</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>As resource personnel for school events, eg fund raising, work experience
programme
<br>
</p>
</li>
</ul>
<ol start="2" data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>To support families</p>
</li>
</ol>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>As advocate of building parent-child bonds
<br>
</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>As champions of parent-child activities
<br>
</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>As mentor to parents
<br>
</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>As resource for parent networking
<br>
</p>
</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Our Regular Activities (during school term)</strong>
</p>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p><strong>Praying Parents@PL</strong>
<br>A faith-filled gathering of Christian parents to pray for the school and
each other. Meets every Monday morning - On-site 7.30am. If you have a
desire to uphold the school in prayer, join the Praying Parents!</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Fathers@School</strong>
<br>A father-centric group set up to encourage active and involved fathering
in PL through purposeful activities and workshops designed to strengthen
father-daughter relationships.</p>
</li>
</ol>
