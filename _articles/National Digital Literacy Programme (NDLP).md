### Purchase of Personal Learning Device (PLD) 2022

As part of the MOE Personalised Digital Learning Programme (PDLP), all Sec 1 students in PLMGS(Sec) will be able to own a Personal Learning Device (PLD) by Term 2 2022. The device chosen by PLMGS(Sec) is the “**Lenovo Chromebook 500e 2nd Gen**” (costing S$518.90). Singapore Citizens will be able to use Edusave to offset the cost of the device.
