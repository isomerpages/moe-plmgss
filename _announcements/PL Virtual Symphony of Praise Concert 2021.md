PL’s biennial Symphony of Praise (SOP) concert is the culmination of months of preparation by our Performing Arts girls for the Singapore Youth Festival (SYF) Arts Presentation.  
  
The concert showcases the accomplishments of our Performing Arts students in the 2021 Singapore Youth Festival (SYF) Arts Presentation and recognise the efforts put in by the trainers and coaches. This is to thank parents for their ardent and strong support for PLMGS (Sec). Most importantly, it is a celebration of the girls’ journey and hard work in the Performing Arts CCA, as the SYF Arts Presentation provided them with an opportunity to hone their artistic talents and to grow together as a team.  
  
In 2021, we organised a virtual concert instead of a physical concert as part of the Safe Management Measures due to the COVID-19 situation. This page is dedicated to the items that were presented at the virtual concert.
