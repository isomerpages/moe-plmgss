<p>The results of the 2025 GCE O-Level Examinations will be released on <strong>Wednesday, 14 January 2025</strong>.</p>
<p><strong>Event Details</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p><strong>Date:</strong> 14 January 2025 (Wednesday)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Time of Release:</strong> 2.00 pm</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Reporting Time:</strong> Students are to be seated by <strong>1.45 pm</strong>
</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Venue:</strong> Shaw Hall</p>
<p></p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Administrative Instructions</strong>
</p>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p><strong>Attire and Grooming</strong>
<br>Students must report in <strong>Full School Uniform</strong>. Please note
that proper grooming standards will be strictly enforced:</p>
</li>
</ol>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>School badge, name tag, socks and shoes must be worn.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Hair must be neat and of natural colour (undyed).</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Only dark-coloured hair accessories are permitted.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Fingernails must be kept short.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<ol start="2" data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p><strong>Settlement of Outstanding Dues</strong>
<br>Students with outstanding school fees, examination fees or library fines
are required to settle these at the General Office <strong>before 1.30 pm</strong> on
the day of the results release.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Collection by Parents/Guardians</strong>
<br>Parents and guardians are welcome to accompany their child/ward to the
school for the collection of results. Parents are advised to park in the
nearby HDB carpark, as no parking is allowed within the school premises.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Collection by Proxy</strong>
<br>Candidates unable to collect their results in person may appoint a proxy.
Proxies must present the required documents for verification. Please contact
the General Office at 6281 6606 if you need assistance with the appointment.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<p><strong>A Message to Our Students</strong>
<br>The school looks forward to welcoming you back. We keep you in our prayers
and are committed to supporting you as you make decisions regarding the
next phase of your education.</p>
