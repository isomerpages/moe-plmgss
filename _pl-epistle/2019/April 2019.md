### ADEX Singapore 2019

<iframe src="https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/e/2PACX-1vQ57JMDYUXZLBBYsWOsPQ6cmmxuGa2KYs--QVuwhhploMftKhzENeNl0gmLBvjB5f4q9181xVThDY4S/embed?start=true&amp;loop=true&amp;delayms=3000" frameborder="0" width="960" height="569" allowfullscreen="true"></iframe>

On 12, 13 &amp; 14 April, our PL Lites had a booth to share their project working in partnership with the National Park Board (NParks) &amp; Taman Jurong CC at the Suntec Singapore Convention &amp; Exhibition Centre.  
  
They educated the public about marine pollution and marine debris like microplastics. By using micro:bits coding technology, the team made and coded a device with a single LED light to collect data like light intensity to determine the 'clearness' of the sea water. They were able to test the turbidity of the sea water as an indication of pollution.

### Joint Promotion Ceremony

<iframe allowfullscreen="true" height="569" width="960" frameborder="0" src="https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/e/2PACX-1vTxsaBQoZVM_FXp677eLEU8Mhllpjx1FV9rLMymEIJ6fqkelo9CLatwqGfS-A6EqRC2kF1Be1RKrvoR/embed?start=true&amp;loop=true&amp;delayms=3000"></iframe>

On 5 April 2019 (Friday) afternoon, the staff from the entire PLMGS (Pri &amp; Sec) family came together for the Joint Promotion Ceremony held in the Agape Concert Hall. The ceremony was filled with songs of praise, as well as, humour. Mrs Quek Li Gek, Principal, PLMGS (Sec), opened the ceremony with a Word of Welcome followed by a prayer by Mrs Joyce Ang, Principal, PLMGS (Pri). After the Singspiration segment, our Guest-of-Honour, Rev Dr David Koh presented the Certificate of Promotion to the officers who have been promoted to GEO 3 and 4 substantive grade. Besides the congratulatory presentation slides, the staff were in for a humorous treat. A video, which was specially produced for this occasion was shown, drawing much laughter from the audience. It was a true testament of the creativity found amongst the PL teachers. To end the celebration in the true PL style, all guests and staff were invited to the PL Manna (School Canteen) for a sumptuous spread of afternoon tea.&nbsp;
