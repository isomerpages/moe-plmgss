**Created for His Glory** _(Theme Song of PL Musical 2016)__  <br>
Written by Suzanne Choo and Amy Tham__  <br>
Sung by Claudia Lai, Samantha Ho and Cheryl Hoe_  


Lord we look to You this day <br>
We trust in Your love <br>
You have been faithful to us <br>
Through the seasons of life <br>
As we run in this race <br>
You have provided Your grace

  

Pre-chorus:

So we put our hope in You <br>
And we praise and worship You

  

Chorus:

Let us set our eyes on You <br>
As we trust in Your Word <br>
You are our refuge and hope <br>
Return us to the cross <br>
As we live out our faith <br>
Remind us that we <br>
Are created for Your glory

  

Lord we thank You for this school <br>
And the people You have raised <br>
We rest in Your promise to us <br>
In Your faithfulness we know <br>
You will see us through the storms <br>
You are our tower of strength

  

Pre-chorus

  

Chorus

  

Bridge <br>
May our school be a light <br>
That will glorify His name

  

Chorus

Remind us that we <br>
Remind us that we <br
Remind us that we <br>
Are created for His glory
